Nebicard 5 (nebivolol 5 mg) is used mainly to **treat high blood pressure (hypertension)**.

It is also used for:

* **Heart failure** (mild to moderate)
* **Reducing heart rate**
* **Preventing heart-related complications** like stroke or heart attack

It works by **slowing the heart rate and relaxing blood vessels**, which lowers blood pressure.






If your blood pressure is **low (hypotension)**, you should **talk to a doctor first** before taking anything.

**Midodrine**

**Fludrocortisone**
